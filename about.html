About US

Who Are We?

Located in the heart of Southport, Rai's Sekuwa Corner brings the bold, smoky flavors of traditional Nepali street food to Australia. We blend generations of family recipes with fresh ingredients and warm hospitality.

Our Story

It started with a passion for authentic taste. Rai's Sekuwa Corner began as a small family venture and has grown into a beloved community spot for those craving comfort, spice, and the rich taste of home. We focus on open-fire grilling, time-tested spices, and community-focused service.
