Our Story

Welcome to Rai Sekuwa Corner

Located in the heart of Southport, Rai Sekuwa Corner brings the bold, smoky flavors of traditional Nepali street food to Australia. Our sekuwa is grilled fresh to order, using family recipes passed down through generations. Whether you're craving succulent chicken, juicy pork, or tender mutton, we promise an explosion of flavor in every bite. Come experience the warmth of Nepali hospitality and the unforgettable taste of open-fire barbecue, just like back home.
